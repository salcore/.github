# SalCore AI - The UoS’s Processing Unit Of AI

## Who We Are

SalCore AI is a student-led, project-focused Artificial Intelligence society at the University of Salford. We are a structured technical community dedicated to building real, end-to-end AI systems through collaborative development. We are the UoS’s processing unit of AI 😎.

SalCore AI operates with the mindset of a small applied lab:

- We build and iterate  
- We explore research when it strengthens our systems  
- We uphold engineering discipline through documentation, experimentation, and peer collaboration  

**Our goal:** We exist to cultivate technically grounded, research-aware, and industry-ready AI engineers.

---

## Our Mission

Our mission is to cultivate a project-driven AI engineering community that enables students to:

- Build real AI projects  
- Develop strong engineering habits, including version control, experimentation, documentation, and structured collaboration  
- Engage with research in a practical, implementation-focused manner when needed  
- Contribute meaningfully to open-source and public technical work  
- Grow into confident and technically capable AI practitioners prepared for internships, research roles, and industry positions  

---

## Our Vision

Our vision is to establish SalCore AI as a recognised hub for applied AI engineering at the University of Salford. We envision a sustainable and structured society that:

- That members become capable of building, evaluating, and responsibly deploying modern AI systems  
- Develops members from beginner contributors into technical leads  
- Encourages research-informed development alongside practical implementation  
- Contributes to the broader AI ecosystem through open-source collaboration  
- Builds a reputation within and beyond the University for engineering excellence and technical seriousness  

---

## SalCore AI - Core Pillars

### 1. Applied AI Engineering

SalCore AI is built around the development of real, end-to-end AI systems. We emphasise:

- Structured, team-based projects  
- Clean system design  
- Model evaluation and iteration  
- Documentation and reproducibility  
- Deployment where appropriate  

---

### 2. Research-Informed Development

We engage with research when it strengthens our systems. Rather than separating “research” from “projects,” we integrate them. This includes:

- Implementing selected research papers  
- Benchmarking and experimentation  
- Understanding architectural decisions  
- Analysing trade-offs in model performance  
- Exploring emerging AI techniques responsibly  

This pillar ensures: We understand what we build, not just how to build it. It prevents shallow engineering.

---

### 3. Open Collaboration and Public Contribution

Members learn collaborative workflows, code review practices, and disciplined version control. We encourage:

- Open-source contributions  
- Public GitHub repositories  
- Transparent documentation  
- Responsible knowledge sharing  

---

### 4. Professional Development: Branding and Employability

Technical skill alone is not enough. Engineers must also learn to:

- Communicate clearly  
- Present solutions confidently  
- Define their area of expertise  
- Position themselves professionally  

---

## Internship and Employability Focus

Through our structured pillars, members will:

- Gain real project experience  
- Develop strong technical foundations  
- Build publicly visible work  
- Learn professional communication  

---

## Who This Society Is For

SalCore AI is designed for students who:

- Are serious about building AI systems or exploring research-informed development  
- Seek structured growth and progressive skill development  
- Are committed to disciplined learning, consistent improvement, and taking ownership of their work  

---

## Prerequisites

- Strong interest in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, or Data Science  
- Fundamental proficiency in Python (or willingness to develop it)  
- Basic understanding of mathematics, statistics, and algorithmic thinking  

---

## Our Culture

SalCore AI fosters a culture that prioritizes:

- Depth over shortcuts: We focus on mastery, not superficial tutorials  
- Collaboration over competition: Projects and knowledge are shared; members learn from one another  
- Consistency over hype: Regular, disciplined practice and iteration drive growth  
- Applied engineering over abstraction: We implement, test, and deploy real systems  
- Responsibility and ownership: Members are accountable for their contributions and the quality of the work
